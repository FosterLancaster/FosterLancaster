Terms

Our community guidelines are for the well-being and safety of our supporters and audience. Failure to comply forfeits your legal permission to access our products and services.

1. No Covert Surveillance

Monitoring the activity of our customers and supporters is prohibited. Harassment and phishing are not tolerated and are a breach of your permission to engage with our content.

2. No Controlling Behavior

We support individual autonomy and healthy connections. Restricting communication or dictating access is not allowed.

3. No Discriminatory Exclusion

Any content aimed at isolation based or not based on race, nationality, religion, or family ties is strictly forbidden.

4. No Tolerance for Hate Speech

Hate speech immediately voids your permission to utilize our content and services. Even coded language, euphemisms, and symbols that present racist ideologies are explicitly prohibited.
